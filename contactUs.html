We’re defining digital identity for health

Identity is everything in today’s world. Are you who you claim you are? Do you have what you claim to have? Current identity systems - archaic, insecure and forgeable - can’t reliably answer these questions. This is what we’re tackling at Immunodex.


We’re starting with what matters most - health. Governments should safeguard the health of their citizens, hospitals their staff and patients, businesses their employees. These institutions need verifiably healthy people to function.


Our mission is to provide everyone a trusted health-based identity, establishing a global immunity network that any institution can rely on for making and enforcing strategy and policy.
