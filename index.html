Why current solutions don't work

Invasion of privacy

Contact-tracing apps meant for mass surveillance compromise individual privacy and confidentiality. There is no transparency on how collected data is being manipulated, posing legal and ethical issues.

Fraud & misguided incentives

“Immunity certificates” that grant work and social freedom for positive-tested individuals can be forged and altered. They may also create incentives for deliberate self-infection.

Lack of standardization

Public health uncertainty and jurisdictional heterogeneity mean that employers are defacto responsible for ensuring employee & customer safety while reopening.

Trust is the only long-term solution

Immunodex helps you trust your employees' health and vice versa
so you can resume business safely.

Verifiable Health Passport (VHP)

The VHP is a set of trusted, tamperproof credentials about an employee's health that employers can verify to grant them access to work and interact in physical environments.

Privacy-preserving

Our user-centric design adheres with principles of the EU GDPR and Pan-Canadian Trust Framework. Users hold, own and control their data, exchanging it with other parties only upon consent.

Secure & fraud-proof

Decentralized identifiers of credential holders and issuers with peer-reviewed cryptographic techniques ensure your employees really are compliant as they claim.

No more paperwork

Ensure that health assessments are unalterable and completely auditable. Data profiles can be tailored to your organization's objectives or requirements.

Rapid implementation

Any camera-enabled device can issue and verify VHP credentials, and our technology can be readily whitelabelled.

Coordinate user flow

Streamline the scheduling, reminding and follow-up of health assessment and verification appointments. Availabilities can be adapted to changes in user volume and acuity.

Decentralized network

We use a globally distributed self-sovereign identity network with a blockchain registry to ensure privacy-preserving credentials.

User-friendly design

For your organization, the VHP runs as a secure mobile or tablet app with an easy-to-use interface. Information is exchanged by simply scanning QR codes.

First, an employee books a doctor's appointment.

An authorized employee books an appointment for a health assessment or diagnostic testing (as mandated by your organization or law) with a physician on Immunodex. Our app reminds them with location and time prior to the reservation.

Next, the physician performs the assessment & issues the credential

After onsite check-in at the healthcare provider's, the employee is assessed by a physician or tested, creating a record in their VHP. The outcomes are sent directly to the VHP, with any "doctor's orders" if needed.

3

The employee's VHP is ready for inspection

With their consent, you can now inspect your employee's VHP, exchanging information via a QR code and authorizing them to proceed with the task at hand.
