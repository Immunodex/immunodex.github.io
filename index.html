Features & Functionalities

Verifiable Immunity Passport (VIP)

Securely deliver a patient’s Covid-19 PCR and antibody test results to their VIP on Immunodex. We use this to generate a trusted, tamper-proof credential that can be used to validate their fitness to work or travel.

Coordinate Patient Flow

Streamline the scheduling, reminding and follow-up of testing appointments. Availabilities can be adapted to changes in patient volume and acuity.

Accurate Epidemiological Monitoring

Ensure that data from from mass screening programmes is unalterable, with traceability to origin. Data profiles can be tailored to specific objectives or requirements.

Rapid Implementation

Implement our plug-and-play technology at pandemic speed. Any camera-enabled device can issue and verify credentials.

How it Works

Requesting an immunity credential on Immunodex is easy

1

First, a patient makes a testing appointment

An authorized patient books a testing appointment on Immunodex. Our app reminds them with location and time prior to the reservation.

Learn More
2

Next, administer the test & issue the credential

After onsite check-in with an healthcare provider, the patient gets tested, creating a record in their VIP. Once test results are confirmed, the record gets updated with a specific health status and directives.

Learn More
3

The patient’s credential is ready for inspection

With the patient’s consent, an inspector checks their health status by scanning a QR code on their VIP, authorizing them to proceed with the task at hand.

ID’s can be faked. With stakes so high, how can anyone be trusted?

We solve this using blockchain.

Immunodex ensures that all transactions are secure and unalterable with the Pedersen commitment scheme.

  • Self-sovereign, meaning consent-based and privacy-preserving. Specific data can be anonymized via zero-knowledge proof cryptography

  • Option to digitally sign with revocable ring signatures for higher privacy protection

  • Safeguard against theft and fraud with complete traceability in data provenance

  • GDPR-compliant (we’re working towards HIPAA compliance) Download Whitepaper
